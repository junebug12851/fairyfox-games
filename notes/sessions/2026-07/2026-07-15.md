## PLANT: new game Reprise — call-and-response / recall (v0.23.0)

**Automated PLANT run (`fairyfox-games-new`).** Shipped the 14th game, **Reprise**, on a
**genuinely new verb**: *recall / call-and-response* (memory). Every existing verb (steer,
time-a-catch, thrust, flip-match, aim-bounce, precision-stack, keep-aloft, balance, mirror,
judge-power, swing/grapple, pour/fill, sort/route) is real-time reflex; none asks the player
to **remember and repeat**. Reprise does — Simon-clean on the surface, with the collection's
depth standards baked in from birth.

**The design.** Four pads flash a **call**; you **echo** it (tap / keys 1–4). Land the call →
it grows +1 and the next plays faster; wrong pad → −1 life (3), and the call **replays** so a
slip is recoverable. **Depth = tempo:** the call plays at a beat; echoing **on the beat** is
*in-tempo* (bonus + multiplier ×2…×9), an off-tempo-but-correct press is *safe* (scores,
breaks the multiplier). The precise play is the greedy play — the Polarity scoring shape,
retargeted to memory+rhythm. A run of in-tempo echoes → **Resonance** (double score, gold).
Varied structure = stage-gated **phrases** (Steady/Run calm; Echo/Leap/Mirror/Cascade
notable; **Mirror = greed window**). Stages Prelude→…→Finale + secret **Encore**. No-plateau
tempo asymptote; length caps at LEN_MAX so tempo carries the endless ramp.

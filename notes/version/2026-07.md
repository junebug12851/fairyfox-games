## v0.13.0 — 2026-07-04

**New game: Symmetry — a mirror-steer game.** The ninth game and a mechanically-distinct
verb (mirror coordination). One control, the *spread*, moves two catchers symmetrically
around a centre line; orbs fall on both sides and a catcher only grabs an orb on its lane.
Because the pair mirrors, you often can't save both sides at once — a forced tradeoff that
is the whole game. Gold-ringed **twins** fall as a matched pair (one spread catches both,
for a bonus) — the skill-rewarding counter-play. Miss three orbs and the run ends; orbs
fall faster and thicker by stage.

- **`games/symmetry/`** — pure core (normalised lanes/spread/`y`, no DOM/canvas/timers,
  seedable RNG), external render-shell module + boot-failure fallback, `icon.png`, README,
  package.json. Full Growth stack: stages (Mirror → Reflection → Twin → Kaleidoscope →
  Singularity) + HUD chip + field tint + stage beat; a catch **combo**; meta
  (`symmetry.meta`, 9 skill-safe badges, run-report + near-miss line, legacy
  `symmetry.best` preserved). **23 tests** (incl. the frame-one guard, twin all-or-nothing
  bonus, and a determinism check). Chrome-previewed, no console errors.

## v0.10.1 — 2026-07-02

**Polarity — core made fun (precision-combo).** Owner playtested v0.10.0 and it was still
boring: the stages/badges were a scoreboard *around* a monotone loop (flip-to-match, mostly
wait, get faster). This reworks the loop itself so the second-to-second decision has teeth.

- **Late-flip multiplier (risk/reward).** Landing a *needed* flip at the last instant is a
  **precise** hit and grows a multiplier (×2…×9); flipping early to play safe **breaks it to
  ×1**; a gimme (already matching) is neutral. Every gate you clear scores the current
  multiplier — so `score` (points) now rewards nerve, while `cleared` (gates) drives
  difficulty/stages. Play safe for a trickle or ride the edge for a huge score; one bad read
  ends the run.
- **Escalating variety + density.** Gate generation is now **stage-patterned** (`spawnGate`):
  higher stages alternate more (more forced flips), tighten spacing, and throw bursts. Brisker
  baseline speed + closer gaps kill the early dead time.
- **Feel.** A big **multiplier readout** that heats up with the combo, pops on a precise hit,
  and flinches on a break; precise hits punch the flash/shake. Game-over card shows gates
  cleared + best multiplier. Copy rewritten to teach "flip late, not early".
- **Still pure + tested.** All new scoring/pattern/meta logic is pure core; tests rewritten,
  **36 green** for Polarity (**170 green** across the collection). Previewed live in Chrome
  (no console errors, clean landscape + portrait HUD). `polarity.best` still honoured.

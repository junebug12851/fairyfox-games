Cookies Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

The short version. The Fairy Fox Games collection does not use cookies — none at all, and no tracking. It saves your best scores in your browser’s local storage so the games can remember them. That data stays on your device and is never sent to us.

This Cookies Policy explains how Fairy Fox Games (“the Service”) uses cookies and similar storage technologies. It applies to the games collection at games.fairyfox.io (also served at fairyfox.io/fairyfox-games/), one project within the wider Fairy Fox site.

We do not use cookies

The Service sets no cookies. We use no analytics cookies, no advertising or remarketing cookies, no third-party tracking cookies, and no session cookies. Because there are no non-essential cookies, there is nothing to consent to and no cookie banner to click through.

What we use instead: local storage

To remember your best scores and small display preferences, the games use your browser’s local storage. This is similar to a cookie in that it is saved in your browser, but unlike a cookie it is never automatically sent to any server — it simply lets a game show your best score the next time you play. This information stays on your device. We never receive it. For more, see our Privacy Policy.

Third-party requests

The Service serves its own fonts and does not load them from Google Fonts or any other third-party font service, so no font-related request leaves to a third party. The games embed no ad networks, analytics scripts, or social widgets. The only third parties involved are our hosting providers (GitHub Pages and Netlify), which process basic request data to deliver the site, as described in our Privacy Policy.

Managing local storage

You can clear the data the Service stores at any time by clearing your browser’s site data / local storage for the Service. Your browser’s help pages explain how (for example, Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge). Note that clearing it also resets your saved best scores.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookies Policy as the Service changes. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above. If we ever introduce cookies or analytics, we will update this page first and obtain consent where the law requires it.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this Cookies Policy, contact us at fairy@fairyfox.io.

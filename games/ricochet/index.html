Ricochet
Aim with your mouse (or finger), then click, tap, or press Space to fire. Your shot bounces off the walls — sweep up the glowing targets in its path. Bank one shot through several for a big chain.
Tip: a shot that collects nothing costs a life — three misses ends the run. Aim off the walls, not just straight at a target: the targets shrink as you score, so the easy line-of-sight shots dry up and banking becomes the whole game. Sweep two or more in one shot for a bank — and a bank is worth far more than the same targets picked off singly (a 3-bank scores 6, not 3), so greed pays. Climb the stages (Rookie → Marksman → Trick shot → Bank master); feats earn badges that stick across runs.

A slab slides above your tower. Click, tap, or press Space to drop it onto the slab below. Only the overlap stays — the overhang is sliced off, so every sloppy drop leaves you a narrower target. It slides faster the higher you build.
Tip: a flush drop (dead-on) keeps the full width and pays double — and a run of flush drops pays an escalating bonus, so chaining perfects is where the big towers come from. Climb the stages (Foundation → Mid-rise → High-rise → Spire); feats earn badges that stick across runs.

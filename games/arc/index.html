Ranging
Arc
Hold to build power, release to lob the shot. It always fires at 45° — the only skill is judging how far the pad is and charging just enough to land on it.
Tip: watch the power bar and learn the feel — a near pad needs a short charge, a far one a long one. Land in the pad's bright centre for a bullseye (double points), and keep landing to grow your combo multiplier. A miss breaks the streak and costs one of three lives. Pads arrive in shifting range formations (Ladder, Bracket, Groove, Reach…) that vary every run and open up as you climb, so no two runs range the same. Pads shrink through the stages (Ranging → Volley → Barrage → Siege → Dead-eye); feats earn badges that stick across runs.

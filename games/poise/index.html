Steady
Poise
Tilt the beam to keep the ball balanced. Roll it over the glowing target to score — then chase the next one, without letting the ball slip off either end.
Controls: ← → or A D to tilt, or hold and slide left/right of centre on touch. Tip: lead the ball with small, early tilts — counter its speed before it drifts, don't chase it late; the ball keeps its momentum through a catch. Targets arrive as routes — a Pendulum sweep, a Cradle of easy hops (take them), a Feint, a Creep out to the lip, The Brink — and climbing the stages (Steady → Wobble → Sway → Pitch → Tempest) opens meaner ones, so no two runs walk the same path. Feats earn badges that stick across runs.

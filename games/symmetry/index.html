Mirror
Symmetry
One control, two catchers — locked in a mirror. Move your pointer left or right (or the arrow keys); the pair spreads apart or draws together around the centre line. Catch the falling orbs on both sides.
Because the catchers mirror, you often can't save both sides at once — read ahead and choose. A gold-ringed twin falls as a matched pair: line up one spread and you catch both for a bonus. Missing an orb costs a life; three misses ends the run. Every run is built from shifting cadences, and climbing the stages speeds the fall and unlocks the meaner patterns. Feats earn badges that stick across runs — and a perfect mirror hides more than it shows.
